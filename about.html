Our Historic Pathway

How we expanded from a single family counseling clinic to a nationwide telemedicine and multi-disciplinary healthcare leader.

2012

The Foundation Clinic

MEDTRUST began as a focused psychological counseling service in New York, specializing in behavioral modifications and couple support.

2017

Clinical Expansion

We expanded our healthcare network by incorporating general practitioners, clinical cardiologists, and pediatric departments.

2021

Telehealth Revolution

Launched 24/7 video diagnostics dispatching. Allowed remote patients instant clinical intake via mobile web triages.

2026

The Modern Vision

Operating 12 active physical medical facilities, with over 800 certified specialists and AI-matched clinical assignments.
